CHURRO

INGREDIENTS

3 tbsp butter

2 cups water

2 tbsp sugar

â…› tsp salt

2 cups flour

Oil for frying

METHOD

1. Add water in a pan along with butter, sugar and salt

2. Once it comes to a boil, take it off heat and add in the flour while mixing continuously

3. Cook it again for 2-3 mins until the dough comes together and forms a film on the bottom of the pan

4. Transfer it to a piping bag with a large nozzle once cooled down and no steam is coming out

5. Pipe directly in the pan and fry for 3-4 mins or until golden brown

6. Roll in the cinnamon sugar when the churros are still hot.

7. Serve with a rich chocolate sauce and enjoy!
