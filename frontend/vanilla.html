VANILLA CAKE- EGGLESS

INGREDIENTS

2 + ¼ (270g) all-purpose flour

1 ½ teaspoon (6g) baking powder

1 ½ cup (428g) yogurt

1/2 teaspoon (3g) baking soda

1 + ¼ cup (280g) caster sugar

¾ cup (180ml) vegetable oil

1 ½ teaspoon (7ml) vanilla extract

METHOD

1. Preheat the oven.

2. In a mixing bowl, combine vegetable oil, yogurt, and sugar. Beat the mixture thoroughly until it's well incorporated. Add vanilla extract to enhance the flavor.

3. In a separate bowl, sift together the dry ingredients: flour, baking powder, and baking soda.

4. Gradually incorporate the dry sifted ingredients into the wet mixture. Alternatively, you can add the dry ingredients and milk in stages to ensure a smooth batter.

5. Once the batter is smooth and well-mixed, introduce the vinegar (OPTIONAL:- put coffee powder in hot water(3ml) and add it to the batter). This will activate the leavening agents and contribute to the cake's texture.

6. Prepare the baking pan by greasing it with oil and either dusting it with flour or lining it with parchment paper to prevent sticking.

7. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius (356 degrees Fahrenheit).

8. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.

9. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 20 minutes. Alternatively, you can use the skewer test: insert a skewer into the center of the cake; if it comes out clean, the cake is ready.

10.Once baked to perfection, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool before serving.

VANILLA CAKE - EGG

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 cups plain flour (maida)

1 1/2 tsp baking powder

1 cup powdered sugar

3/4 cup butter

1 cup powdered sugar

3 eggs

6 tbsp milk

1 tsp vanilla essence

METHOD

1. To make rich vanilla sponge cake, sieve the plain flour and baking powder in a deep bowl and keep aside.

2. Cream the butter in a bowl with an electric beater for 1 minute.

3. Add the sugar and cream it with an electric beater for 2 to 3 minutes till it is light and fluffy.

4. Add one egg, 1 tbsp of the sifted flour mixture and beat with an electric beater till the egg is just mixed. Make sure to not over beat the egg.

5. Repeat step 4 two more times.

Add the remaining flour, vanilla essence and milk and fold gently using a spatula till well mixed.

Beat with an electric beater for 30 seconds to mix uniformly.

Line and grease a 175 mm. (7") diameter cake tin and pour the batter into it.

Bake in a pre-heated oven at 180ºc (360ºf) for 50 minutes.

Keep the rich vanilla sponge cake aside to cool slightly, demould and use as required.
