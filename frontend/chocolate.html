Chocolate cake- EGGLESS

INGREDIENTS

1 ½ cup (180gms) maida

¾ cup (90gms) cocoa powder

½ tsp (3gms) baking soda

1 tsp (4gms) baking powder

¾ cup (180ml) vegetable oil

1 ½ cup (300gm) caster sugar

1 cup (285gms) curd

½ tsp vanilla extract

½ cup (120ml) milk + 1tsp (5ml) vinegar

METHOD

1. Preheat the oven.

2. In a mixing bowl, combine vegetable oil, yogurt, and sugar. Beat the mixture thoroughly until it's well incorporated. Add vanilla extract to enhance the flavor.

3. In a separate bowl, sift together the dry ingredients: flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda.

4. Gradually incorporate the dry sifted ingredients into the wet mixture. Add the dry ingredients and milk in stages to ensure a smooth batter.

5. Once the batter is smooth and well-mixed, introduce the vinegar (OPTIONAL:- put coffee powder in hot water(3ml) and add it to the batter). This will activate the leavening agents and contribute to the cake's texture.

6. Prepare the baking pan by greasing it with oil and either dusting it with flour or lining it with parchment paper to prevent sticking.

7. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius (356 degrees Fahrenheit).

8. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.

9. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 20 minutes. Alternatively, you can use the skewer test: insert a skewer into the center of the cake; if it comes out clean, the cake is ready.

10.Once baked to perfection, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool before serving.

CHOCOLATE CAKE - EGG

INGREDIENTS

5 tbsp cocoa powder

1 1/4 cups plain flour (maida)

1 1/2 tsp baking powder

3/4 cup butter

1 cup powdered sugar

3 eggs

6 tbsp milk

1 tsp vanilla essence

METHOD

1. Sieve the plain flour, baking powder and cocoa powder in a deep bowl and keep aside.

2. Cream the butter and sugar in a bowl, mix well using a spatula till smooth. Keep aside.

3. Add the eggs, one at a time and mix gently with help of a spatula.

4. Finally, add the plain flour- cocoa powder mixture, vanilla essence and milk and fold gently using a spatula (OPTIONAL:- put coffee powder in hot water(3ml) and add it to the batter).

5. Grease and dust a 175 mm. (7") diameter cake tin and transfer the batter into it.

6. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 200°c (400°f) for 30 minutes.

7. Keep the chocolate sponge cake aside to cool slightly. Demould and use as required.
